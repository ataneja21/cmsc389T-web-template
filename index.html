Projects

Internship at Qualcomm

In Summer 2023, I was an Embedded Engineering Intern at Qualcomm! This was an amazing experience! I got to work on the Android Display Team and test to see if the display was corrupted or not.

Here are some more technical details about that:

  1. I helped detect corruption to the screen during a "Trusted UI Session" or during a time where you have something very sensitive/personal on your phone, such as when you're looking at your bank account.
  2. I tested on devices with one and two screens, so I helped run tests for the Samsung Galaxy Fold, which has two screens!
  3. I worked in C++ and ran CRCs (Cyclic Redundancy Checks) to help write my tests!
